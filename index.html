Step by Step Guide

Process to get job easily
with Job Wallah

Follow the below steps from creating profile to schedule job interview

  • #1 Create Profile

    Download our app 'JobWallah' from PlayStore. Open App > Go to Create Account > Enter all Details > Choose Password > Submit to Create Account.

  • #2 Apply for vacancy

    Go to 'Vacancy' section and find your department jobs. Check job details of your department and click Appky Now. Now wait for 24 hours to get a call.

  • #3 Schedule Interview

    If you apply for right vacancy, Job Wallah team will call you and ask for confirmation. If agree, then team will schedule interview with company and thats it.

  • #4 Get Job

    Attend your interview with compnay and get selected. If not, then find new vacancy on App.
